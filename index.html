About me

Hello! I'm Adam and I'm currently based in Tokyo. I'm a web developer particularly focused on the Front End where I strive to build engaging applications that deliver satisfying user-experiences. When I'm not coding I'm usually out exploring Tokyo on my bicycle, reading, or studying Japanese.


Having worked online as an English teacher I've always felt the need to provide my students with gainful tools in their language-learning journey, thus a lot of what I'm building has been geared towards that end.


To be creative in my work and to be challenged by the process of problem-solving are key drivers in my passion for web development.
